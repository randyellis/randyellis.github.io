FREQUENTLY ASKED QUESTIONS

Need some help understanding how Envoi truly works? Here's some of our most commonly asked questions.

Q: How much does Envoi cost?

A: Envoi is a monthly pay-as-you-go service where agreement coverage starts at $49.99 a month for a $5,000 contract. We also offer annual protection plans at a discount.

Q: Is there a contract commitment?

A: No, with Envoi you start when you want and you can cancel at any time.

Q: Are you a legal service?

A: No, Envoi is a protection service that gives your business the reassurance your contracts won't go unpaid. We offer that by providing a network of legal professionals who can review and advise you as to what your legal recourse should be.

Q: What is Envoi's relationship to me during and after a signed agreement?

A: After an agreement is signed, Envoi is then committed to giving you the security that your contract is legally re-enforced by our network of legal professionals. In the event a contract is not fulfilled by yourself or a client, Envoi will serve as the advisor to help resolve the conflict swiftly.

Q: I was awarded a judgement to be paid, but I don't know how I will collect?

A: Collecting an award can be more challenging than going to court. Being an Envoi member, we can offer cash advances to keep your business cash-flow positive while we pursue the original court judgment.

Q: Do you offer debt recovery services?

A: Yes! As part of your Envoi membership. We work with an affiliate network of debt recovery providers that can recover and broker any non-paid invoices. This can boost recovery rates and preserve client relationships by empowering those in debt to easily and responsibly address their obligations.
